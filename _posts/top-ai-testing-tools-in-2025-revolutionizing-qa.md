## Must-Have Features in AI Testing Tools

When selecting AI testing tools, consider capabilities that align with your QA goals.

### Key Features:
- **Test Automation:** Streamline repetitive manual tasks to maximize efficiency.
- **Self-Healing Scripts:** Handle minor UI changes autonomously, reducing script maintenance.
- **Predictive Analytics:** Leverage past data to foresee failures before they pose risks.
- **Regression Testing:** Identify areas needing retesting to prevent new bugs.
- **Natural Language Processing:** Simplify test creation with human-readable cases and automatic conversion.
- **Scalability:** Support extensive testing across diverse platforms and environments.
