- **Predictive Analytics**: AI predicts potential vulnerabilities in code, transforming testing from reactive to proactive.

- **Continuous Testing in DevOps**: Enhancing CI/CD pipelines with AI-driven real-time feedback and faster resources allocation.
- **Test Data Generation**: AI produces comprehensive, anonymized datasets for greater data coverage and regulatory compliance.
