## Understanding Regression Testing and Its Challenges

Regression testing verifies that newly implemented features or patches do not interfere with already functioning code. It ensures stability during software updates but comes with its own set of hurdles:

### Key Challenges:

1. **Time-Intensive**: Running extensive test suites can significantly slow down continuous delivery pipelines.
2. **High Costs**: Large test suites coupled with skilled testers mean higher operational costs.
3. **Coverage Gaps**: Dynamic applications often outpace traditional testing frameworks, leaving critical areas untested.
4. **Human Errors**: Manual processes result in inaccuracies and inefficiencies.

Addressing these challenges calls for automated, smart solutions—and AI is leading the charge.
