## What Are Autonomous AI Testers?

Autonomous AI testers are intelligent systems designed to execute software testing tasks with minimal human intervention. Unlike traditional automated tests, they utilize machine learning to dynamically generate, execute, and optimize tests. Their advanced capabilities include issue identification, user behavior prediction, and continuous learning.

### Key Features of Autonomous AI Testers

1. **Self-learning:** Adapts to evolving project requirements.
2. **Dynamic test generation:** Creates real-time test cases.
3. **Universal adaptability:** Supports mobile, web, cloud, and IoT testing.
4. **Bug prediction:** Identifies and predicts anomalies before runtime.

These features transition QA teams from reacting to problems to preventing them, elevating software quality and customer satisfaction.
