### Early Beginnings
Initially, AI’s role in QA testing was limited to automating repetitive tasks, scripted test execution, and streamlining regression testing. These early tools reduced manual workload but lacked adaptive capabilities for dynamic, complex systems.

### AI Growth in the 2020s
Machine learning and deep learning technologies gained momentum during the 2020s. The introduction of smarter tools allowed QA teams to analyze expansive datasets, uncover hidden patterns, and predict defects as they emerged. Comprehensive AI-driven platforms began addressing edge cases and optimizing processes in real time.
