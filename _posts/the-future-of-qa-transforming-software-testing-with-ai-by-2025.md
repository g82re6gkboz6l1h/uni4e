#### 1. Scriptless Automation Testing
AI automates test creation without coding, boosting efficiency and adaptability to app updates.
#### 2. Predictive Analytics
Using historical testing data, AI predicts potential defects, enabling risk-based testing.
#### 3. Continuous Testing in DevOps
AI integrates QA into CI/CD pipelines, identifying issues in development phases and driving faster rollouts.
