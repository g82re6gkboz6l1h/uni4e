## Understanding Predictive Testing with AI

Predictive testing uses machine learning to anticipate software behavior, optimize test cases, and prevent failures before testing starts. Key AI advantages include identifying bug-prone code areas, suggesting tailored strategies, and streamlining resources. This approach ensures faster, error-free releases.
