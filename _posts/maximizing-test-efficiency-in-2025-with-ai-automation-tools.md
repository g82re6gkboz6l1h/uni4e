What is Model-Driven Testing (MDT) and How Does AI Elevate It?

Model-driven testing (MDT) empowers teams to automate testing processes by developing abstract system representations. AI further enhances MDT by rapidly generating realistic, comprehensive test scenarios that ensure optimal coverage.

### Why Should QA Teams Adopt MDT?
- AI automates complex testing scenarios efficiently.
- MDT provides real-world system behavior simulations to uncover weaknesses early.
- It reduces late-stage defect corrections, saving development costs.

With tools like Zof AI, QA teams can streamline performance, automate test generation, and receive predictive insights from fault simulations.
