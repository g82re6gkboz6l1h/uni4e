## What is AI Predictive Testing?
Predictive testing uses AI algorithms to forecast defects and risks by analyzing historical data and detecting patterns. Unlike traditional methods, AI offers scalability, enhanced anomaly detection, and continual refinement of processes to ensure software runs seamlessly. It’s a groundbreaking strategy for Agile teams to preemptively address vulnerabilities and deliver better software, faster.
