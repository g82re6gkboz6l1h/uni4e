## Understanding AI-driven Test Automation

AI-driven test automation harnesses artificial intelligence to enhance and simplify testing processes for software applications. Unlike traditional tools that rely on predefined scripts, AI-powered platforms dynamically adapt to code changes, analyze patterns, predict issues, and facilitate smarter testing strategies with minimal manual input.

By leveraging machine learning, natural language processing, and predictive analytics, AI tools alleviate repetitive tasks, detect critical flaws, and expand test coverage, evolving into adaptive systems that learn continuously.

## Evolution of Automated Testing With AI

Automation tools have journeyed from basic record-and-playback systems to sophisticated, AI-integrated platforms. The initial focus was primarily restricted to reducing workload by automating repetitive scripts for user interfaces and APIs, but their static nature limited their performance.

Introduction of AI algorithms shifted the paradigm, enabling predictive insights, handling more complex testing scenarios, and even performing exploratory tests typically reserved for manual methods.
