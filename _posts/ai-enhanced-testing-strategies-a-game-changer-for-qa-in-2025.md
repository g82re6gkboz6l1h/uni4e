## How AI Revolutionizes Adaptive Testing and Risk-Based Assessment

AI's capacity for learning and analysis makes it uniquely suited for adaptive testing and risk-based assessments—two essential areas of modern QA.

1. **Adaptive Testing**: AI-driven algorithms analyze historical and real-time application behavior to prioritize test cases that are most likely to uncover defects. Over time, these systems improve, optimizing efficiency. For example, in regression testing, AI needs to run only a specific, targeted subset of tests, significantly reducing time without sacrificing test comprehensiveness.

2. **Risk-Based Assessment**: AI's predictive analytics can evaluate which aspects of a software system are at the highest risk of failure, allowing developers and testers to allocate resources where they matter most. State-of-the-art tools like **Zof AI** offer predictive models that calculate operational risk for new releases and refine test plans dynamically to match a project’s specific requirements.
