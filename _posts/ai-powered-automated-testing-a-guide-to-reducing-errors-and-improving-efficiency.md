## Common Pitfalls of Manual Testing

### 1. **Error Prone Processes**
Manual testing frequently leads to oversights due to repetitive tasks, highlighting the need for automation.

### 2. **Scalability Constraints**
Testing on various devices and browsers quickly becomes unmanageable without automation.

### 3. **Resource Heavy**
Testing manually consumes valuable time and workforce, restricting flexibility and innovation.

### 4. **Insufficient Coverage**
Manual approaches often skip edge cases, exposing gaps and vulnerabilities.

### 5. **Complex Defects Overlooked**
Identifying multi-faceted bugs or API malfunctions manually is challenging, emphasizing the demand for AI.

Automated testing ensures complete coverage from the front-end to APIs and backend systems.
