### Challenges in Achieving Test Coverage

#### 1. **Incomplete Test Scenarios**: Complex applications make manual test scenario creation overwhelming, leaving gaps and vulnerabilities.
#### 2. **Time Constraints**: Tight deadlines force prioritization, leading to partial testing coverage.
#### 3. **Dynamic Systems**: Frequent feature updates ensure test cases quickly become obsolete.
#### 4. **Data Challenges**: Limited access to diverse and privacy-compliant data hampers comprehensive testing.
#### 5. **Human Errors**: Manual processes introduce biases and oversight.
