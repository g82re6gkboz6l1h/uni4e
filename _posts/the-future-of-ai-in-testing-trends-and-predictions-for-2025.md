## Emerging Trends Reshaping QA Automation

1. **Self-Healing Test Scripts** save time by adjusting scripts dynamically to UI changes.
2. **AI-Powered Test Data Generation** ensures realistic, diverse datasets for better edge-case testing.
3. **Continuous Testing in CI/CD** pipelines speeds development cycles with real-time regression coverage.
4. **Shift-Left Testing** identifies risks early in development.
5. **Computer Vision Algorithms** analyze UI performance for flawless designs.
