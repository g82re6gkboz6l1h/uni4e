## What is Continuous Testing?

Continuous testing involves running automated tests consistently across the software development lifecycle. This allows developers and testers to identify issues early, accelerating delivery while maintaining optimal quality standards. Unlike traditional QA processes, continuous testing aligns with the iterative nature of DevOps workflows. 

### AI and Continuous Testing: A Perfect Match
AI is perfectly positioned to complement the demands of continuous testing. Machine learning and AI technologies thrive in analyzing patterns, automating repetitive tasks, and delivering predictive insights.
