## What Is Generative AI & NLP?

Generative AI creates data, designs, and innovative solutions based on input training, while NLP specializes in understanding and processing text-centric environments. Combined, they revolutionize how software documentation, requirement gathering, and test scenarios are approached. These technologies automate defect identification, craft intelligent test cases, and even simulate varied user behaviors.

Advanced AI models, such as GPT, push beyond typical automation tasks by enabling system vulnerability prediction, analysis of complex requirements, and seamless adaptation to iterative updates at unprecedented speed.

- **Static Processes**: Traditional automation struggles with dynamic adaptability in complex testing environments.

Generative AI & NLP overcome these barriers by enabling:
- **Adaptive Test Scripts**: AI creates and updates scripts in real-time, seamlessly adapting to updated systems.
