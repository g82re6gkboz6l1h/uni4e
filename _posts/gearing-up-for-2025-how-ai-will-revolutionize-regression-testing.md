## What is Regression Testing and Why is it Important?
Regression testing ensures that updates to software—be it new features, patches, or upgrades—don’t inadvertently introduce bugs to existing functionality. By running test cases under various scenarios, developers secure the integrity and reliability of their applications. 

### Challenges of Manual or Traditional Regression Testing
Regression testing has always been vital but comes with significant hurdles:

- **Time Burden:** Testing hundreds or thousands of cases manually takes weeks. Even automated systems often fall short of speeding things up.  
- **Costly Overheads:** Prolonged cycles drain both financial and human resources. 
- **High Maintenance:** Evolving test suites require constant tweaks, leading to inefficiencies. 
- **Prioritization Issues:** Teams sometimes focus on less-critical areas and miss high-risk zones, leaving vulnerabilities untested. 

Faced with these inefficiencies, AI-powered tools offer solutions to improve regression testing exponentially.
