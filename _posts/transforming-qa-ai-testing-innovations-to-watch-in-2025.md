## Overview of AI's Evolution in Software Testing Up to 2025

AI gradually integrated into software testing, with steady growth over the past decade. Today, AI tools excel at automating tasks, analyzing datasets, and predicting failures.

### Milestones that Shaped AI Testing:

1. **2015-2020:**
   - Automation tools like Selenium dominated QA workflows.
   - Early AI integrations focused on speeding up repetitive testing tasks.

2. **2020-2023:**
   - Predictive AI became prevalent, identifying patterns to forecast vulnerabilities.
   - Machine learning optimized testing cycles through historical bug analyses.

3. **2023-2025:**
   - [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) introduced advanced AI frameworks with capabilities like autonomous testing, dynamic code repairs, and real-time learning.
   - AI expanded into self-healing systems, intelligent regression testing, and predictive analytics.

These advancements enable QA teams to deliver faster, more reliable results while dramatically reducing costs. To remain competitive, AI testing must address dynamic challenges and adapt to ever-evolving ecosystems.
