## What is Autonomous Testing and What Role Does AI Play?

Autonomous testing leverages AI technologies like machine learning (ML), natural language processing (NLP), and predictive analytics to independently design, run, and evaluate test cases. Unlike traditional testing, autonomous testing reduces manual workload and delivers faster, high-quality results. Tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) are at the forefront, automating processes like test case generation and defect detection to keep pace with increasing software demands.

- Suggest new test cases for high-risk functionalities.

Platforms like Zof AI utilize these capabilities, enabling proactive fixes and transforming QA from reactionary to preventative, saving time and resources.
