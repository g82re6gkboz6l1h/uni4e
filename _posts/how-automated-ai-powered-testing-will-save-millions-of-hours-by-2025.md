AI-powered testing redefines QA processes by bringing self-learning capabilities and predictive analytics to the forefront. Unlike manual approaches, AI adapts to updates and detects patterns, simulating human-like decision-making processes to optimize testing. Beneficial features include:

- **Self-healing test scripts**: Automatically adjusts test cases to accommodate software changes.
- **Predictive analytics**: Pinpoints potential failure points through historic data.
- **Test prioritization**: Focuses on critical areas in applications to maximize efficiency.

This approach streamlines workflows, speeds up delivery timelines, and maintains unmatched quality control.
