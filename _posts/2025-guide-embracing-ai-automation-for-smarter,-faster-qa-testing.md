## The Future of QA Testing: Why AI Automation Matters

The software industry is entering a new era, where AI-based solutions redefine QA testing. Legacy testing frameworks depend heavily on manual scripts and human intervention, making them slower and prone to errors. 1. **Speed**: AI tools diagnose bugs and performance flaws faster than humans, slicing development time without sacrificing quality.
2. **Accuracy**: Machine learning eliminates human errors, detecting vulnerabilities with unparalleled precision.

4. **Cost Efficiency**: It streamlines testing, reducing reliance on large teams while scaling processes effectively.
