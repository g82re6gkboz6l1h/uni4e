## Why AI is Crucial for Agile Testing

Agile development demands fast, reliable testing, but manual processes often lead to bottlenecks. AI tools are bridging the gap:

**1. Speeding Up Testing Cycles:** 
AI automates tedious tasks like regression testing, code reviews, and performance analysis, ensuring continuous delivery without delays.

**2. Earlier Bug Detection:**
Predictive AI pinpoints bugs in real-time, helping teams tackle issues before they escalate, reducing costly setbacks.

**3. Smarter Test Coverage:**
AI systems analyze data quickly, optimizing test priorities to focus on riskier areas, ensuring speed and thorough testing.
