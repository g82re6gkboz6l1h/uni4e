1. **Automated Test Generation**

Self-healing capabilities eliminate script breakdowns caused by software changes, simplifying maintenance significantly.<br>

Top-tier tools integrate effortlessly with various DevOps platforms, cloud services, and devices for better adaptability.

5. **User-Focused Interfaces**  
Designed for simplicity, even non-technical users can use these platforms effectively, bridging technical gaps.
