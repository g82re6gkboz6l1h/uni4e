## Challenges in Traditional QA Systems

1. **Time & Resources**: Manual testing demands extensive effort and is unsuitable for rapid timelines.
2. **Human Error**: Repetition often leads to oversight, compromising reliability.
3. **Scalability Issues**: Testing across diverse systems manually is impractical.
4. **Dynamic Adaptations**: Traditional methods strain under frequent updates and ecosystem changes.
