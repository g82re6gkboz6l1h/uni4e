Static approaches to test execution can bog down teams as software grows in complexity. AI delivers predictive test execution—a smarter approach leveraging historical test data, code trends, and user patterns to prioritize high-risk areas and likely failure points.

### Benefits of Predictive Test Execution:
- **Time Efficiency:** Focus only on critical tests.
- **Improved Accuracy:** Minimized errors due to dynamic selection.
- **Optimized Resource Usage:** Cut down unnecessary test cycles.

For instance, Zof AI analyzes historical trends to pinpoint essential areas in regression testing, accelerating testing phases and identifying defects earlier.
