## Addressing Ethical Pitfalls in AI Testing
The immense capabilities of AI in QA—such as performance optimization and predictive analysis—bring equally substantial concerns. Chief among them is accountability. When AI fails or produces biased results, who is responsible? The persistent “black box” issue, the opaque nature of AI decision-making, exacerbates the challenge.

Bias in datasets, ethical governance, and the need for human oversight become critical as organizations deploy AI testing frameworks at scale. This blog covers actionable strategies for balancing automation with human input, ensuring systems are built on ethical foundations.
