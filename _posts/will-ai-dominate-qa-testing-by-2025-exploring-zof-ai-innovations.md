Challenges to AI’s Full QA Domination

While the future looks promising, AI adoption in QA isn’t without hurdles:

1. **Dependency on Human Insight:** AI excels in automation but lacks human creativity and contextual understanding.
2. **Security & Ethics:** Managing sensitive data with AI requires stringent policies to ensure safety.
3. **Skill Gaps:** Adequate training in AI and QA tech is essential for the workforce.
4. **Bias in Data Models:** AI relies on historical data, which may carry unintended biases.
5. **Legacy System Integration:** Transitioning from outdated systems to AI platforms like Zof AI can be challenging.
