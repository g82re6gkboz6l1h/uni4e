## How AI Redefines QA Testing Processes

### 1. Dynamic Test Case Creation
AI generates adaptive test cases without requiring manual updates, ensuring relevance as the software grows in complexity.

### 2. Advanced Bug Detection
AI and machine learning (ML) detect vulnerabilities by analyzing historical bug patterns, identifying anomalies proactively.

### 3. Continuous Regression Testing
AI automates regression test suite updates, enabling fast releases without compromising quality.

### 4. Predictive Analytics for Risk Mitigation
AI uses historical testing data to determine defect hotspots, enabling QA teams to allocate resources smartly.
