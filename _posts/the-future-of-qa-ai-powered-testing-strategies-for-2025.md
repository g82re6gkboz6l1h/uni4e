### 3. Smarter, Targeted Regression Testing
AI improves regression testing efficiency by prioritizing critical cases, leveraging data from previous runs to save resources.

AI ensures robust testing by identifying gaps and testing edge cases often missed by manual efforts, enhancing application functionality across diverse scenarios.
