### **The Evolution of QA: Manual Efforts to AI-Powered Automation**

Manual testing, once the bedrock of QA, is now being replaced by automated solutions that streamline efficiency. #### **3. Real-Time Test Metric Analysis**
AI systems analyze test data in real time, offering actionable insights and recommendations for performance improvements.
