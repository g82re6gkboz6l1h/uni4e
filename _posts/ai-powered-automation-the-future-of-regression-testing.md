## Regression Testing: Why It’s Essential for Software Development
Regression testing is designed to guarantee that new changes (fixes, features, or updates) do not compromise existing features of an application. This ensures:

- **Seamless User Experience**: Prevents interruptions or frustrations for users.
- **Reliable Applications**: Confirms stability even after updates.
- **Cost-Effective Development**: Detects issues early, reducing the effort and expense of late-stage fixes.

With growing system complexity, manual regression testing is slow and increasingly obsolete. AI integration is easing this bottleneck.
