## What is Hyperautomation in QA?

Hyperautomation goes beyond basic automation by integrating advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML). In the QA domain, it brings intelligent frameworks capable of enhancing testing speed, precision, and scalability.

Unlike traditional automation, hyperautomation introduces context-aware testing that mimics human logic and decision-making. Armed with AI, it identifies test cases, predicts defects, resolves issues, and adapts to fast-changing development environments effortlessly. 

With agile and DevOps methodologies emphasizing continuity and speed, hyperautomation ensures QA processes reduce manual intervention while simultaneously increasing accuracy. As enterprises transition to complex ecosystems, these AI-driven frameworks deliver precise results at unmatched speeds, driving modern digital transformations.
