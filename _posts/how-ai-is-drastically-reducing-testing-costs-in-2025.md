### The Cost of Traditional Software Testing and AI’s Transformative Role

Software testing has always been a cornerstone of maintaining quality. However, traditional methods come with hefty price tags due to labor dependency, time-intensive regression testing, late-stage bug hunting, and limited scalability. AI offers solutions by automating labor-intensive tasks, identifying defects early, predicting vulnerabilities, and optimizing testing cycles.

**Key points include:**
1. Manual testing demands high labor costs and considerable time.
2. Regression testing becomes increasingly inefficient with application evolution.
3. Discovering bugs at later stages escalates expenses drastically.
4. Scaling modern software with diverse integrations and APIs is resource-intensive.

AI addresses these pain points by enabling automation, predictive analytics, and rapid scalability, effectively streamlining testing processes.
