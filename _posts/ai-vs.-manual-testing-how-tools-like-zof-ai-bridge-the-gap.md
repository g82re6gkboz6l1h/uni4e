## The Shift from Manual to AI-Assisted Testing

### Challenges of Manual Testing:
- **Time-intensive** for complex applications.
- Prone to **human error**.
- **Scalability issues** with large projects.

### Rise of AI Tools:
AI tools address these issues by learning from data, adapting to changes, predicting outcomes, and executing tests at scale.
