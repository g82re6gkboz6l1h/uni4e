## AI Models Driving Predictive Testing

At the core of predictive testing are sophisticated AI algorithms designed to anticipate testing outcomes with remarkable accuracy. These include:

### 1. Machine Learning (ML) Regression Models
Analyzes historical data to predict defect-prone software areas using methods such as regression analysis and random forests.

### 2. Neural Networks
Simulates human intelligence to determine intricate patterns in complex datasets, offering insights on likely system bottlenecks.

### 3. Natural Language Processing (NLP)
Processes user requirements to identify ambiguities and misalignments, minimizing specification risks.

### 4. Anomaly Detection
Identifies deviations in typical software behavior, exposing potential vulnerabilities before they escalate.

### 5. Reinforcement Learning
Creates self-improving AI models that evolve over time for increasingly precise predictions.
