## Key Advancements in AI for QA

### 1. **Self-Healing Test Automation**
AI tools dynamically adapt test scripts to software updates, reducing maintenance efforts and enhancing scalability.

### 2. **Defect Prediction with Machine Learning**
AI leverages historical data to highlight high-risk areas of code, enabling efficient resource allocation and early issue detection.

### 3. **Automated Test Case Creation with NLP**
Natural language processing converts user stories into comprehensive test cases in seconds.

### 4. **AI-Assisted Exploratory Testing**
Discover edge cases and hidden defects through AI-driven exploratory testing strategies for deeper coverage.

### 5. **AI-Driven Continuous Testing**
Integrating AI into CI/CD pipelines ensures real-time feedback, minimizing release cycles and safeguarding quality.
