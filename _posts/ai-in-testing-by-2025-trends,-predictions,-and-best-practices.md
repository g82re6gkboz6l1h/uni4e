## 2025 Trends in AI Testing
- **Personalized User-Centric Testing**: AI simulates user-specific patterns for tailored QA.
- **Self-Healing Automation**: Intelligent systems repair broken test cases dynamically.
- **Real-Time Performance Monitoring**: AI identifies and fixes bottlenecks proactively.
- **Predictive Analytics**: Prevent defects early, optimizing costs and workflow efficiency.
- **Ethical AI Standards**: Transparent and unbiased results ensure trust and compliance.
