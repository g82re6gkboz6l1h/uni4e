## Key Challenges for QA Teams Embracing AI

1. **Lack of expertise** in AI technologies like machine learning and NLP.
2. **High implementation costs** for revamping infrastructure.
3. **Resistance to change** among teams used to traditional methods.
4. **Bias risks** in AI models due to flawed training data.
5. **Balancing human-AI roles**, as both are crucial for effective testing.
