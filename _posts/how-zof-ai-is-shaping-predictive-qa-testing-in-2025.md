### What Is Predictive QA Testing?

Predictive QA testing combines AI, historical data, and behavior analytics to detect and prevent software vulnerabilities. By analyzing user patterns, system data, and past testing results, predictive QA ensures software issues are addressed before they impact end users. Benefits include:

- Early bug identification, reducing costly fixes.
- Enhanced stability in dynamic environments through adaptive models.
- Insights into technical debt, enabling efficient risk management.
