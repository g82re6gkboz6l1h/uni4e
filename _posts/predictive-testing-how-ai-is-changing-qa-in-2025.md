## What is Predictive Testing and Why It Matters?

Predictive testing leverages AI algorithms to identify vulnerabilities early in the development cycle—long before issues surface in production. It marks a shift from reactive bug-fixing to proactive quality control.

With the complexity of modern software systems driven by APIs, microservices, IoT, and AI models, predictive testing plays a crucial role. Bugs escaping into production erode user confidence and inflate maintenance costs.
