## Why AI in Software Testing?

### Bridging Manual and Automation Gaps
AI enables QA teams to move beyond manual testing inefficiencies and rule-based automation. By leveraging machine learning (ML) algorithms and predictive analytics, AI tools can analyze patterns, identify anomalies, and even create automated tests tailored to dynamic software ecosystems.

### Benefits AI Brings:
- Speed up release cycles for Agile-focused workflows.
- Reduce errors compared to manual QA scripts.
- Scale test runs for massive datasets.
- Predict defects and prevent rework.
