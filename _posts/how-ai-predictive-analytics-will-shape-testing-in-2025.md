## What Is Predictive Analytics in Software Testing?

Predictive analytics leverages AI, machine learning (ML), and big data to forecast potential software vulnerabilities and testing priorities. By analyzing historical defect patterns and system interactions, it empowers QA teams to identify areas at risk for bugs, streamline testing efforts, and improve overall efficiency.

#### Key Benefits:
- **Error Prediction**: Spot vulnerabilities early.
- **Test Optimization**: Focus resources strategically.
- **Resource Efficiency**: Optimize testing workflows.
- **Proactive Prevention**: Address issues preemptively.
