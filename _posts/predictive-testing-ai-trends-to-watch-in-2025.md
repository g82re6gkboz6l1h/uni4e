## What is Predictive Testing?

Predictive testing leverages AI to anticipate potential bugs and system flaws before they disrupt user experiences. Using historical data, usage patterns, and development insights, this process proactively identifies vulnerabilities, reshaping QA practices from reactive to proactive. Embracing predictive testing provides:

- **Faster debugging cycles:** Minimized time identifying bugs.
- **Improved QA reliability:** Elevated accuracy ensures smoother user experiences.
- **Scalability:** Efficient handling of complex systems and large data volumes.
