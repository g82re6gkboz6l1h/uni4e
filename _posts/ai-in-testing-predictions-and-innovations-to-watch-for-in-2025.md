## AI Trends and Advancements to Anticipate by 2025

- **Self-Healing Test Scripts**: Automatic script updates as software evolves.
- **Autonomous Testing Tools**: Mimicking human exploratory testing.
- **Real-Time Defect Prediction**: Rapid identification and resolution of bugs.
- **AI-Driven Shift-Left Testing**: Early-stage quality enhancement and defect prevention.

Machine Learning (ML) in Predictive Testing

ML supports risk-based testing, coverage optimization, anomaly detection, and adaptive test suites to preemptively eliminate defects before deployment.

QA Professional Evolution in an AI World

AI will empower manual testers, shifting their focus toward strategic activities, collaboration, and oversight. Upskilling in AI technologies will be critical for career growth.
