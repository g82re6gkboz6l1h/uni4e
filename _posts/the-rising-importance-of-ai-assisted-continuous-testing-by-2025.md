## What is AI-assisted Continuous Testing?

Continuous testing ensures quality across every development cycle stage by employing automated tests. When AI joins the mix, it builds upon this practice, powered by capabilities such as intelligent algorithms and adaptive learning, ensuring greater efficiency.

### Unique Benefits of AI-assisted Continuous Testing

1. **Intelligence**: It uses pattern recognition and predictive analytics to enhance test prioritization.  
2. **Automation**: Routine tasks are automated, freeing QA for advanced scenarios.  
3. **Adaptability**: AI adapts dynamically to new requirements, maintaining relevance.  
4. **Efficiency**: Tests focus on high-risk areas, cutting redundancy.  

By seamlessly combining intelligence and automation, AI transforms how risks are identified and mitigated in software development.
