### 1. **Applitools**
- **Focus**: Visual testing and User Interface (UI) accuracy.
- Known for its visual regression testing capabilities, Applitools specializes in catching subtle design inconsistencies. Its strength lies in UI and front-end testing.

### 2. **Test.ai**
- **Focus**: Basic automated test creation.
- An easy-to-use tool for simplifying test creation. However, it may lack the advanced customization and scalability required for larger projects.

### 3. **SeleniumAI**

- Mabl’s standout feature is its self-correction mechanism for automated test scripts adapting to code changes.
