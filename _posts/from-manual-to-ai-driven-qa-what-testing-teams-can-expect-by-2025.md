## The Evolution Toward AI-Powered QA

Historically, QA relied on manual processes that were accurate yet time-consuming, followed by automation frameworks like Selenium and Appium that improved regression testing but still required heavy human involvement. AI-driven QA eliminates inefficiencies through self-learning algorithms, predictive analytics, and autonomous defect identification. Tools like Zof AI empower teams to adapt AI solutions for scalability, meeting modern demands like Agile and DevOps methodologies.

### 2. **Defect Detection & Risk Prioritization**

AI tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) identify issues at speed and prioritize critical defects, reducing downtime.

### - Cost Implications
Initial adoption costs may deter smaller organizations.

### - Resistance to Change
Teams trained in manual or traditional automation may hesitate.
