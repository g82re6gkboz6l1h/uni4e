## What is Predictive Quality Assurance?

Predictive QA marks a shift from reactive to proactive testing processes. It utilizes AI algorithms to anticipate errors during development, saving time and resources while enhancing software quality. Powered by tools like Zof AI, predictive QA uses historical data and intelligent modeling, ensuring software resilience in an increasingly complex tech landscape.
