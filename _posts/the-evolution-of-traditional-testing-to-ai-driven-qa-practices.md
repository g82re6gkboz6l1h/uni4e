## A Brief History of Software Testing

Software testing has undergone a significant evolution, categorized into three critical stages that reflect the changing needs of the industry:

1. **Manual Testing**: Early QA relied entirely on skilled testers who manually executed test cases and analyzed system behavior. Although effective for bug identification, this method was time-intensive, error-prone, and often inefficient.

2. **Automated Testing Frameworks**: As software complexity grew, tools like Selenium and JUnit automated repetitive tasks and improved consistency. However, these frameworks required technical expertise and couldn’t predict new bugs or analyze test data patterns.

3. **AI-Driven QA**: The integration of AI and machine learning into QA tools, such as [Zof AI](https://zof.ai), marks a breakthrough in testing. Unlike traditional automation, these tools offer predictive analyses, dynamic self-learning, and advanced test optimizations.
