## What is Autonomous Testing Powered by AI?

Autonomous testing utilizes AI and machine learning to fully automate the software testing lifecycle. Unlike manual testing or predefined scripts, AI-driven systems dynamically adapt to software changes, analyze historical data, and independently execute tests. This self-sufficient approach eliminates inefficiencies and delivers faster, more reliable results.
