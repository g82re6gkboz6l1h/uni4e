## What Is AI in Software Testing?
AI in software testing leverages technologies like machine learning, natural language processing, and predictive analytics to automate and optimize testing processes. Unlike traditional automation tools requiring predefined scripts, AI tools learn and evolve. They adapt to changing software designs and user behaviors, turning QA into a proactive, intelligent process.
