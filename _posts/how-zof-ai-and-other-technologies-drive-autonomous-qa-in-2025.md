## What is Autonomous QA?

Autonomous QA is the use of artificial intelligence (AI) to independently oversee and manage the software testing process. It goes beyond traditional QA by automating not only test execution but also test designs, debugging, and self-healing capabilities. This results in a self-reliant QA system, offering:
- **Higher Accuracy**: AI minimizes human errors.
- **Improved Efficiency**: Faster testing and feedback loops enable agile development.
- **Scalability**: AI handles massive datasets across diverse platforms effortlessly.

With platforms like Zof AI, independent QA is becoming a scalable, reliable, and cost-effective solution for businesses globally.

## The Limitations of Traditional QA

Traditional quality assurance methodologies often fall short of meeting today’s demands due to various inefficiencies:

- **Heavy Reliance on Manual Testing**: Time-consuming and prone to errors.
- **Low Adaptability**: Brittle scripts struggle with system and UI changes.
- **Slow Feedback**: Delays in resolving software issues hinder agile sprints.
- **Scalability Challenges**: Testing global apps for various devices and geographies.
- **High Expense**: Resource-heavy processes strain development budgets.

### The Need for Advancement
