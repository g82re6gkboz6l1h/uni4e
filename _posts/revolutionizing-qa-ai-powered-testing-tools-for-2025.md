Take regression testing, for example—traditional setups demand excessive resources while AI-powered engines prioritize critical scenarios, significantly enhancing efficiency and accuracy.

Technological innovations, such as test case generation, coverage analysis, and predictive defect detection, are making AI indispensable to QA teams. Platforms like Zof AI showcase how end-to-end testing evolves into a seamless, intelligent process.
