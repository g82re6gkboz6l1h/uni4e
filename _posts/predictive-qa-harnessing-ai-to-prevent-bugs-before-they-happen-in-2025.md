## What is Predictive Quality Assurance with AI?

Traditional quality assurance (QA) identifies bugs already present in a program using manual or automated tests. While effective, this method is **reactive**, addressing issues only after they are introduced.

**Predictive QA**, however, flips the paradigm by detecting vulnerabilities and anticipating potential issues during the development process. AI-powered systems analyze historical data, such as past bug reports and testing outcomes, to proactively predict errors and provide actionable insights to developers. By highlighting at-risk code areas or patterns, these tools allow teams to focus preemptively, significantly reducing post-release bugs and development inefficiencies.
