## The Paradigm Shift: AI Transforming Test Automation Processes

Traditional test automation has long been a staple in agile development, yet it faces limitations like dependency on static workflows that crumble under unforeseen scenarios. AI eradicates these weaknesses by incorporating intelligence and adaptability into testing.

For instance, AI-enhanced test automation utilizes machine learning to enable self-healing scripts, reducing dependency on manual intervention during application updates. Furthermore, AI optimizes beyond repetitive tasks by testing complex scenarios that traditional methods find cumbersome. By 2025, AI's involvement will revamp traditional test automation by introducing unparalleled efficiency and adaptability.
