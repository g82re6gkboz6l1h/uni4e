Striking the Balance: Human Testing vs AI Automation

### Why Manual Testing Still Matters
Manual testing remains essential for areas requiring human insight—UI aesthetics, accessibility compliance, cultural biases, and nuanced user experiences. Human QA testers ensure alignment with business requirements where AI may generalize outcomes incorrectly.

### How AI Elevates QA
AI technologies manage logic-intensive scenarios like regression testing and system faster glitch identification. Tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) automate complex processes, ensuring scalability without sacrificing accuracy.
