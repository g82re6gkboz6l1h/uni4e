Conversational AI leverages natural language processing (NLP) to interact seamlessly with humans via text or voice commands. By simplifying workflows—like quick report generation or test case creation—this innovation empowers QA engineers to perform efficiently without extensive coding expertise.

### Key Role of Conversational AI in QA:
- Streamlined communication using human-like interaction.
- Automated test case creation through natural language processing.
- Accelerated debugging and collaborative reporting.
