## Continuous Testing Explained: From Automation to AI Evolution
Continuous testing involves automating tests throughout the software development pipeline to ensure consistent quality. Originally reliant on simple automation, it faced challenges like scalability issues and flaky tests. Over time, CI/CD enhanced test integration and analytics capabilities.

With AI and ML entering the picture, these limitations are disappearing. Predictive models, anomaly detection, and self-healing test scripts allow teams to optimize workflows, reduce execution time, and improve accuracy for faster releases.

## Key Benefits of AI and ML in CI/CD Pipelines
AI and ML are reshaping continuous testing with transformational capabilities:

1. **Optimized Test Execution**: AI prioritizes key tests, cutting redundant efforts and saving time.
2. **Anomaly & Defect Detection**: ML algorithms uncover hidden errors, reducing risk.
3. **Dynamic Script Healing**: Automation adjusts itself to UI and API changes.
4. **User Behavior Insights**: AI creates relevant test cases modeled after real-world usage.
5. **Advanced Reporting Tools**: Dashboards powered by ML highlight actionable trends, defects, and risks.
