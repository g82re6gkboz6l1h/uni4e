## The Bug Detection Bottleneck

### Key Challenges in Traditional QA
Bug detection remains a complex and time-consuming aspect of development:

1. **System Complexity**: Modern apps involve interconnected components, creating hard-to-pinpoint bugs.
2. **Scaling Testing Efforts**: Feature-rich apps require extensive testing, overwhelming manual workflows.
3. **Human Error**: Fatigue and bias in manual testing lead to missed issues.
4. **Time Constraints**: Agile/DevOps cycles demand faster iteration, often limiting thorough testing.
