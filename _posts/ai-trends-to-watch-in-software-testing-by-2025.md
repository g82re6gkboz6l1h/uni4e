Self-Healing Testing Powered by AI

Managing test scripts with dynamic applications is now easier, thanks to AI-led self-healing capabilities. This innovation ensures reduced maintenance efforts.

- Recognizes renamed fields or updated workflows and modifies scripts.
- Creates checkpoints leveraging historical data records.
- Automatically fixes broken tests or signals QA professionals to act.
