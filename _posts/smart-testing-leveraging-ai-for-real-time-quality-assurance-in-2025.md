- **Scalability**: Efficiently manages large projects with complex codebases.
- **Accuracy**: Detects subtle vulnerabilities often missed manually.
- **Adaptability**: Dynamically learns and improves with each iteration.
- **Cost-efficiency**: Minimizes reliance on manual testing.
