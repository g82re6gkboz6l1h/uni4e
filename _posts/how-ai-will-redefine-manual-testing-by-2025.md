## Manual Testing vs. AI Testing
1. **Human Judgment vs. Machine Learning:** AI excels in data analysis; humans in intuitive UX evaluation.
2. **Exploratory Testing:** Creativity-led by humans; AI supplements with data-driven insights.
3. **Efficiency:** AI outpaces manual testing in speed and scalability, ideal for regression testing.
4. **Error Recognition:** Technical bugs are AI's strength; usability flaws remain human-led.
