## What is Predictive AI in QA Testing?

Predictive AI in QA testing utilizes machine learning, data analytics, and artificial intelligence to proactively identify potential software failures before they happen. In contrast to conventional methods that detect issues post-development, predictive AI employs historical data, past bug patterns, and codebase insights to anticipate vulnerabilities.

This forward-thinking approach enables developers to address problems in the early stages of the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC), reducing errors, time, and costs. With the ability to predict real-world software behaviors, simulate scenarios, and detect hidden faults, predictive AI enhances automated testing to new levels of accuracy and efficiency.
