### The Challenges of Traditional Bug Detection

1. **Manual Debugging Takes Time** - Debugging can take hours, slowing down productivity.
2. **Human Error Risks** - Mistakes in manual testing often lead to misdiagnosed or unresolved issues.
3. **Missing Critical Context** - Bug reports frequently lack detailed insights, wasting developer time.
4. **Collaboration Bottlenecks** - QA, developers, and managers often face miscommunication, delaying fixes.

As software grows more complex in architectures like microservices or AI, outdated bug detection methods struggle to keep up. Fortunately, **AI innovation is the solution**.
