## What Is Human-Centric Testing?

Human-centric testing prioritizes the end-user experience, focusing on usability, accessibility, and emotional connection with a product, going beyond standard bug detection and technical compliance. In this methodology, AI acts as an enabler rather than a replacement for human testers—streamlining repetitive tasks to free up time for human creativity and intuition. 

**Key Features of Human-Centric Testing:**
- Enhancing exploratory testing with AI-generated insights.
- Assessing UX design for accessibility and user satisfaction.
- Strengthening ethical and inclusive testing practices.

Finding the Balance: Automation and Human Creativity

While AI accelerates repetitive tasks and tests technical functionalities, preserving the human creativity essential for exploratory and UX testing is crucial. Automation alone cannot replicate human intuition, empathy, or critical thinking—skills necessary to address complex usability and accessibility issues.

**Why Human Creativity Remains Vital:**
- Humans can assess the emotional impact and intuitive design of products.
- Testers can question assumptions and explore unconventional user paths for better outcomes.
- Bias in AI algorithms requires human oversight to ensure inclusivity and fairness.

By 2025, achieving harmony between automated precision and human ingenuity will be the foundation of effective software testing.
