AI can predict and mitigate bugs before they appear, saving time and resources. Here’s how proactive bug detection works:

### • **Data-Driven Analysis**: Past testing data informs predictions of future defects to pinpoint critical areas for focused testing.
### • **Behavior Insights**: Monitoring user interactions and system logs identifies underlying software vulnerabilities before they turn into defects.
### • **Real-Time Monitoring**: Continuous scanning during runtime instantly flags issues, streamlining debugging and ensuring a smoother user experience.

Such capabilities allow teams to move beyond a reactive approach to embrace a more preventative, automated QA process, minimizing post-launch bug issues.
