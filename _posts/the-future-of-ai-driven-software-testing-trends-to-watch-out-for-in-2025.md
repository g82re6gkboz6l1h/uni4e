## Top Trends to Watch in AI Software Testing for 2025

### 1. **Self-Healing Automation**
AI dynamically adjusts test scripts with minimal manual intervention, keeping pace with changing application features.

### 3. **Codeless Test Automation**
Empowering QA teams to create test cases without complex scripting—AI brings automation to non-technical teams.

### 4. **Natural Language Processing (NLP) for Test Development**
