## Tackling Traditional Software Testing Limitations

Years of manually-driven testing processes and rigid automation tools have left software testing with significant challenges:

1. **Time-Intensive**: Manual reporting and script development slow down overall testing processes.
2. **Expensive**: Complex applications demand extensive resources, driving up costs.
3. **Limited Scalability**: Current testing frameworks struggle with massive datasets and diverse configurations.
4. **Missed Edge Cases**: Humans often overlook crucial areas that lead to application errors.

AI solves these challenges with speed and efficiency, marking a pivotal technological shift.
