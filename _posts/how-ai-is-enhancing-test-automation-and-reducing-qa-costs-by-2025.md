## Traditional QA Costs and Challenges

Despite its importance, conventional QA techniques come with inefficiencies that drive up costs and introduce risks. Key challenges include:

### 1. Labor-Intensive Processes
Manual testing often requires large QA teams to design, execute, and maintain test cases, leading to laborious processes that can consume 30-40% of a project’s budget. Errors and inefficiencies in manual testing further amplify costs.

### 2. Limited Test Coverage
Manual testers struggle to achieve comprehensive test coverage under time constraints, increasing the risk of undetected bugs reaching production and causing downtime or customer dissatisfaction.

### 3. High Maintenance Costs for Automated Frameworks
Even automated tools like Selenium require constant scripting maintenance. Rapid software updates can break automated scripts, reducing ROI and creating additional workloads.

**Solution**: AI eliminates inefficiencies through intelligent automation, self-healing scripts, and predictive analytics, solving these long-standing issues.
