Addressing Challenges and Ethical Considerations

Challenges for AI-driven testing include:

1. **Algorithm Bias**: Ensuring unbiased training to maintain software integrity.
2. **Data Privacy Compliance**: Adhering to GDPR and data protection laws.
3. **Over-Reliance on AI**: Balancing automated and manual oversight.
4. **Ethical Transparency**: Clear documentation of AI behavior and data usage.
5. **Reskilling Teams**: Preparing testers for AI-driven workflows with targeted training programs.

Continued focus on these concerns is crucial for building trust in AI-powered systems.
