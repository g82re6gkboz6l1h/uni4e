From machine learning to natural language processing (NLP), cutting-edge AI technologies are revolutionizing how software testing is conducted. Here's a closer look:

### Natural Language Processing (NLP)
AI now converts plain-language specifications into executable test cases. Tools such as Zof AI help QA professionals with minimal coding experience leverage AI's benefits.

### Autonomous Testing
AI systems are deploying self-sufficient testing with reinforcement learning, evolving dynamically and handling complex test scenarios without manual intervention.
