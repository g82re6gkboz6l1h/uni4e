## Introduction: AI's Disruption of Traditional QA Processes

Artificial intelligence has become a catalyst for innovation in software testing, bridging limitations of manual testing and human-driven workflows. AI-powered tools offer a compelling alternative—enabling predictive analytics, autonomous testing, and intelligent defect prediction at scale. AI's innate ability to analyze complex patterns and automate repetitive tasks unlocks significant value for teams, from automated test case generation to vulnerability identification. By freeing testers from mundane workflows, AI delivers both actionable insights and enhanced software reliability.
