## Key Challenges in Current Test Case Management

Traditional test case management often encounters hurdles that slow down development cycles, especially in Agile and DevOps environments. Common challenges include:

- **Time-intensive Processes**: Manual creation and updates require extensive effort.
- **Low Risk Focus**: Critical defects are often missed due to misplaced testing priorities.
- **Adaptability Limitations**: Teams struggle to update test cases for dynamic software changes.
- **Duplicate Test Suites**: Lack of redundancy checks inflates test suites unnecessarily.
- **Traceability Issues**: Connecting requirements to test cases and defects is labor-intensive.

These issues result in missed milestones, inflated budgets, and slower time-to-market.
