### 3. Predictive Analytics for Defect Resolution
AI helps predict failures before users encounter them. Leveraging development history and bug data, tools like Zof AI analyze risks early in the deployment pipeline, cutting post-release corrections.

### 5. Visual and UI Automation Tools
Visual testing automation powered by computer vision ensures thorough user interface validations. Dynamic component detection and visual anomaly checks streamline testing across devices and platforms, delivering better aesthetics and functionality.
