Transitioning From Reactive to Proactive Testing

Reactive testing, focused on bug fixing post-development, fails to meet the demand of agile systems. Proactive testing disrupts this model, leveraging AI to anticipate and mitigate issues in advance.

### AI Drives Proactive Testing:

- **Behavior Prediction**: Forecast system failures using user data.
- **Pre-Deployment Code Reviews**: Detect problematic code seamlessly.
- **Continuous Monitoring**: Reduce risks by monitoring anomalies post-deployment.

Zof AI ensures proactive testing empowers teams to prevent problems instead of solving them later.
