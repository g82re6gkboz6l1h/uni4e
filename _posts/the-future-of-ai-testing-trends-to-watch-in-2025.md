## Key Current Testing Challenges Addressed by AI

1. **Test Case Overload Management**: AI algorithms streamline analysis by identifying redundancies and prioritizing high-value touchpoints.
2. **Adapting to Dynamic Software Environments**: AI-powered self-healing test scripts maintain accurate alignment with changing application features.
3. **Reducing Human Error**: Predictive algorithms ensure bugs and critical oversights are promptly captured.
4. **Cost Efficiency and Labor Optimization**: Advanced automation minimizes extensive manual interventions and long-term operational expenses.
