## Why AI-driven Testing Is the Next Big Leap in QA

For decades, manual testing has been central to software QA, but it comes with inefficiencies—time-consuming repetition, human errors, and limited scalability for complex systems. By 2025, over 80% of software testing activities will be automated, largely thanks to AI tools. Here’s why the change matters.

### Key Benefits of AI-powered Testing

- **Efficiency**: AI can execute thousands of tests simultaneously, saving time and boosting productivity.
- **Accuracy**: Detect subtle issues humans may miss, reducing the risk of release bugs.
- **Scalability**: Enable seamless testing for interconnected systems like APIs and cloud services.
