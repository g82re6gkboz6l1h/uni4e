## Challenges Facing AI-Based Software Testing

Despite these advancements, integrating AI into software testing isn’t without hurdles. Some prominent challenges include:

1. **Reliance on High-Quality Data**: AI models require vast, clean datasets—something many companies struggle to procure and maintain.
2. **Bias Issues in Training**: AI models can inherit biases from historical data, leading to incomplete testing outcomes.
3. **Interpretability of AI Models**: The "black box" nature of AI systems makes their decision-making process opaque to human testers.
4. **Over-Dependence on Automation**: While automation improves efficiency, excessive reliance on AI can diminish the role of human creativity and strategic thinking in testing.
5. **Implementation Costs**: Upgrading to AI-powered tools requires significant investment in software, training, and infrastructure, which can be especially burdening for smaller companies.
