The Current State of AI Testing Tools

Despite being in an exploratory phase, AI testing tools are already delivering groundbreaking solutions. These tools automate repetitive QA tasks, enhance efficiency, and identify risks before they surface. Here’s what AI-powered tools can currently do:

- **Automated Test Optimization**: Dynamic generation and update of test scripts based on code changes, reducing human effort.
- **Predictive Testing**: AI learns from past QA data to identify potential vulnerabilities ahead of time.
