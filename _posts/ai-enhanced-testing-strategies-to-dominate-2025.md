Avoiding Pitfalls When Adopting AI-Driven Strategies in QA

Despite its promise, AI adoption requires strategic planning to prevent inefficiencies and setbacks.

### Pitfalls to Watch Out For

1. **Unrealistic Expectations:** 
   Understand AI’s limitations—tools like Zof AI enhance processes but don’t fully replace human intuition.

2. **Underestimating Training Needs:** 
   Avoid underestimating the time and effort required to upskill QA teams effectively.

3. **Over-Reliance on Automation:** 
   Maintain a balance between automation and manual oversight to catch gaps in testing logic.

4. **Ignoring Data Quality:** 
   Ensure datasets are cleaned and annotated for optimal AI accuracy.

5. **Compatibility Issues:** 
   Evaluate tools like Zof AI for seamless integration with legacy systems.

6. **Failure to Measure ROI:** 
   Establish KPIs to track success, such as reduced testing hours and improved bug detection rates.
