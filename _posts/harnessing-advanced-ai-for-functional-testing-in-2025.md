## What is Functional Testing?

Functional testing verifies whether a software system operates according to its requirements by evaluating output against input scenarios. It focuses on ensuring user-centric features, seamless component integration, and flawless regression testing. However, traditional manual approaches struggle with growing software complexity. Enter AI solutions like Zof AI, which automate and enhance these processes.
