## What is Self-Healing Test Automation?

Self-healing test automation refers to frameworks that automatically adapt to application changes—whether it's broken locators, altered workflows, or structural shifts. Powered by **AI**, these systems negate the need for human intervention in test case repairs, saving time and optimizing delivery schedules.

### Key Features:
- Automatically detects and adapts to changes.
- Reduces testing downtime and maintenance effort.
- Accelerates software release cycles with intelligent predictive insights.
