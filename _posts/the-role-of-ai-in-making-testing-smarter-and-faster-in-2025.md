## How AI Enhances QA Testing

AI transforms the QA lifecycle, from requirement analysis to defect management:

- **Efficient Requirement Analysis**: Automates test case creation, saving time and effort.
- **Redundancy Detection**: Removes outdated test cases to optimize efficiency.
- **Defect Prediction**: Proactively detects potential bugs using historical data.
- **Self-Healing Automation**: Adapts test scripts to UI changes without manual updates.
- **Performance Testing**: Predicts bottlenecks through real-time analytics, improving product reliability.

AI creates proactive, streamlined QA workflows, enabling teams to release software faster and with higher confidence.
