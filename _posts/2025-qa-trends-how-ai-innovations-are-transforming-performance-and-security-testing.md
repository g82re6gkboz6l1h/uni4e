## Performance and Security Testing Reinvented with AI

In 2025, traditional QA approaches are giving way to AI-driven methodologies. Machine learning tools can now simulate complex scenarios, identify security vulnerabilities, and monitor performance continuously for seamless issue detection and prevention. The core focus areas for AI in QA include:

1. **Performance Testing:** AI helps diagnose traffic bottlenecks, latency problems, and system disruptions via real-time data analysis. Predictive monitoring ensures potential issues are addressed proactively.

2. **Security Testing:** With cyberattacks becoming more intricate, AI enhances testing by dynamically scanning vulnerabilities, analyzing attack vectors, and automating penetration tests to protect systems from emerging risks.
